Hi! I am a PhD candidate at the Centre for Human-Computer Interaction Design (HCID) at City, University of London. My recent research involves examining how digital technologies (including apps, data, algorithms and more) shape labour processes and work practices. In my PhD project, I adopt a socio-technical approach to analyse the global labours of AI, ranging from model building to data labelling, and identify opportunities for worker-centric design and policy interventions.
