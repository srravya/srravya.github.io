Srravya Chandhiramowuli Hello! I am a technology researcher and PhD candidate in the Institute for Design Informatics at the University of Edinburgh.

My current research examines the work of data annotation for AI, paying particular attention to systemic challenges and frictions, to envision and inform just, equitable futures of AI.

My research builds on scholarship in Human Computer Interaction (HCI) and Science and Technology Studies (STS) and seeks to shape the design, policies and practices surrounding emerging technologies.
