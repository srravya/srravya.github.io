--- layout: default title: Srravya Chandhiramowuli --- Srravya Chandhiramowuli

I am a PhD candidate in Human Computer Interaction Design (HCID) at City, University of London. Here, I am affiliated with the HCID Centre within the Computer Science department. In my PhD project, I examine the work practices of data annotation for AI, situating them within the global flows of resources, and labour, to identify opportunities for worker-centric design and policy interventions. My broad research interests lie in building and contributing to socio-technical understandings of emerging technologies and their implications for processes and arrangements of labour in various domains.
