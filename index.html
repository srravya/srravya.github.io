--- layout: default title: Srravya Chandhiramowuli ---

Srravya is a PhD student at HCID. Her research interests lie in examining the implications of computing technologies (including apps, data, algorithms and more) for labour processes and work practices. In her PhD project, she adopts a socio-technical approach to analyse the global labours of AI, ranging from model building to data labelling, and identify opportunities for worker-centric design and policy interventions.
